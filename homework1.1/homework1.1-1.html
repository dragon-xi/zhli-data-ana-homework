探测器时间差方法刻度位置¶
    

  • $t_x = t_d - t_u$
    • 

  • $t_{xl} = -11.81$, $t_{xr} = 41.64$ //左、右边界
    • 

  • $t_{xoff} = (t_{xl} + t_{xr})/2 = 14.915$  //中心位置，offset
$$x = \frac{2L}{t_{xr} - t_{xl}}*(t_x - t_{xoff})=3.742*(t_x - 14.915)$$
    •
