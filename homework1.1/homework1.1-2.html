探测器能量比值法刻度位置¶
    

  • $t_q =ln\frac{q_u}{q_d}$
    • 

  • $t_{ql} = -0.522143$, $t_{qr} = 0.528151$ //
    • 

  • $t_{qoff} = (t_{ql} + t_{qr})/2 = -0.003$  //offset
$$x = \frac{2L}{t_{qr} - t_{ql}}*(t_q - t_{qoff})=190.4*(t_x + 0.003)$$
    •
